Ingredients

Explore Our Brownie

Almond Flour
Almond flour is rich on vitamin E and antioxidants which helps to reduce the risk of serious health conditions.
Cocoa powder
Cocoa is rich in mirconutrients and antioxidants, which help on blood pressure or improve cholesterol.
Coconut oil
Coconut oil has anti-inflammatory anti-microibal and anti-fungal properties and may be helpful in the prevention of dental caries.
Eggs
Eggs are a good source of proteins and are rich on important nutrients such as, vitamin B6, B12, and D.
Blueberries
Blueberries are rich on vitamin C and D, it can also help with heart health, bones strength, blood pressure and diabetes.
Soy milk
Soy milk is healthy, plant-based, and can help support healthy muscles and organs. It is rich on omega 3 that reduce risk of dementia and Alzheimer's disease.
